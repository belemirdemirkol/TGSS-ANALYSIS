# Turkish General Social Survey (TGSS) Analysis

This project provides a comprehensive exploratory data analysis (EDA) of the Turkish General Social Survey dataset. It focuses on understanding the demographic distribution and the relationships between gender, age, education level, and urbanization.

## Key Features
* **Data Cleaning & Preprocessing:** Converting numeric codes to meaningful categorical labels.
* **Demographic Analysis:** Detailed examination of age, gender, and education distributions.
* **Bivariate Analysis:** Investigating the relationship between gender and education levels across different urbanization types.
* **Data Visualization:** Using Seaborn and Matplotlib for clear, insightful statistical charts.

## Insights
* **Education:** High school graduates form the largest group in the dataset.
* **Gender:** The dataset is well-balanced, ensuring reliable comparative analysis.
* **Bivariate Trends:** Analysis reveals specific gaps in education levels when segmented by gender and region.

## Tech Stack
* **Python**
* **Pandas** (Data Manipulation)
* **Seaborn & Matplotlib** (Visualization)
* **Jupyter Notebook**
